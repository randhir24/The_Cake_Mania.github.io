FRUIT CAKE RECIPE

A fruitcake is a rich, dense cake typically filled with dried and candied fruits, nuts, and spices, often with a touch of alcohol. It's known for its long shelf life and is traditionally associated with holidays, especially Christmas.

How to make fruit cake:

ingredients:

Here’s a basic list of ingredients for a Fruit cake:
  • Dry Ingredients:
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ½ tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp ground cinnamon
- ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
- ¼ tsp ground allspice
- ¼ tsp salt
  • Fruits & Nuts:
- 1 ½ cups mixed dried fruit (raisins, currants, chopped dates, chopped dried apricots)
- ½ cup candied cherries (halved)
- ½ cup candied citrus peel (optional)
- 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts, pecans, or almonds)
  • Wet Ingredients:
- ½ cup unsalted butter (softened)
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 tbsp molasses or golden syrup
- Zest of 1 orange (optional but adds great flavor)
  • Liquids:
- ½ cup dark rum, brandy, or orange juice (for soaking the fruit)
- Extra alcohol or juice for brushing after baking (optional for aging)

Would you like to know how to prepare it?

Here's a step-by-step recipe to make a delicious Fruit cake!

Fruit Cake Recipe:
  • Step 1: Soak the Dried Fruits
    • - Mix 1½ cups dried fruits, ½ cup candied cherries, and ½ cup citrus peel in a bowl.
    - Add ½ cup dark rum, brandy, or orange juice.
    - Cover and let sit at room temp (or refrigerate overnight)..

  • Step 2: Prep & Preheat
    • - Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C).
    Grease and line a loaf or round cake pan with parchment paper.
    Optional: Wrap the outside of the pan with a strip of brown paper or parchment to prevent over-browning.

  • Step 3: Mix the Batter
    • 1. Cream Butter & Sugar
    - Beat ½ cup softened butter with ¾ cup brown sugar until light and fluffy.
    2. Add Eggs & Flavoring
    - Beat in 3 eggs, one at a time.
    - Add 1 tsp vanilla extract, 2 tbsp molasses, and orange zest if using.
    3. Sift Dry Ingredients
    - In a separate bowl, sift together:
    - 1½ cups all-purpose flour
    - ½ tsp baking powder
    - ½ tsp cinnamon
    - ¼ tsp each: nutmeg, allspice, and salt
    4. Combine Everything
    - Add the soaked fruit (with any remaining liquid) to the creamed mixture.
    - Fold in dry ingredients gently.
    - Stir in 1 cup chopped nuts.

  • Step 4: Bake
    • - Pour batter into the prepared pan and level the top.
    Bake for 1½ to 2 hours, until a skewer inserted comes out clean.
    Check after 1 hour; cover loosely with foil if the top is browning too fast.

  • Step 5: Cool & (Optional) Age the Cake

    • - Cool in the pan for 10–15 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
    For a boozy version:
    - Brush warm cake with extra brandy or rum.
    - Wrap in parchment and foil, then store in an airtight container.
    - You can “feed” it with alcohol weekly for deeper flavor.

  • Step 6: Enjoy!
    • - Slice, serve, and enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate cake!
