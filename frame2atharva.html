Vanilla Strawbery Cake

A vanilla strawberry cake is a delightful, sweet dessert that combines the classic flavor of vanilla with the fruity sweetness of strawberries. The cake itself is typically made with a light and fluffy vanilla-flavored sponge or butter cake base, which provides a soft and airy texture. The cake layers are often paired with a smooth, rich strawberry filling or frosting, sometimes made from fresh strawberries, strawberry jam, or strawberry puree. The combination of the subtle, creamy taste of vanilla and the fresh, tangy burst of strawberry creates a perfect balance of flavors. It can be decorated with fresh strawberry slices on top, or have a strawberry glaze drizzled over it for an extra burst of flavor and color. This cake is often served at celebrations like birthdays, weddings, or summer gatherings, thanks to its refreshing taste and appealing aesthetic.

Ingredients For the Vanilla Cake:
2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
2 ½ tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
1 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
1 ¾ cups granulated sugar
4 large eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup whole milk (room temperature)

For the Strawberry Filling/Frosting:
1 ½ cups fresh strawberries (pureed or finely chopped)
1 cup heavy cream
½ cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract

For the Decoration:
Fresh strawberries (sliced)
Optional: Extra whipped cream or a drizzle of strawberry

For the cake
1. Prepare the Vanilla Cake: Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 8-inch round cake pans or line them with parchment paper. Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, combine 2 cups flour, 2 tsp baking powder, and ½ tsp salt. Set aside. Cream Butter & Sugar: In a large bowl, beat 1 cup butter and 1 ½ cups sugar together until light and fluffy (about 3-4 minutes). Add Eggs: Beat in 3 eggs, one at a time, ensuring each is fully incorporated. Add Vanilla & Dry Ingredients: Stir in 2 tsp vanilla extract. Gradually add thery ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with ½ cup milk. Mix until just combined.
Bake: Divide the batter evenly between the two cake pans and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
2. Prepare the Strawberry Frosting: Whip Cream: In a mixing bowl, beat 1 cup heavy cream and ½ cup powdered sugar with a hand mixer until stiff peaks form. Add Strawberries: Gently fold in 1 ½ cups pureed or chopped strawberries until fully combined. The frosting should be thick and creamy.
3. Assemble the Cake: Once the cakes are completely cool, place the first layer on a serving plate.
Spread a layer of the strawberry frosting on top. Add the second cake layer and frost the top and sides with the remaining frosting. 4. Decorate & Serve: Decorate: You can decorate the top with fresh strawberry slices for a finishing touch.
Serve: Slice and enjoy the light, fluffy vanilla cake with a delicious strawberry frosting!
